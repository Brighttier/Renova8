## What is RenovateMySite?

**RenovateMySite** is an AI-powered business concierge platform that helps entrepreneurs and agencies acquire clients faster. It combines lead generation, brand analysis, website creation, and personalized outreach into one seamless workflow.

### The One-Liner

## The 4-Step Concierge Wizard

Our flagship feature guides users through a complete client acquisition workflow:

### Step 1: FIND (Scout)
**What it does**: AI-powered local business discovery using Google Maps integration
- Search by industry + location
- Get business details, contact info, and website status
- Identify businesses that need a new or better website
- **Cost**: 5 credits ($0.10)

### Step 2: ANALYZE (Brand)
**What it does**: Extract brand identity from existing materials
- Upload logo for color palette extraction
- Analyze typography and visual style
- Generate brand tone and personality guidelines
- **Cost**: 2 credits ($0.04)

### Step 3: BUILD (Website)
**What it does**: Generate complete, responsive HTML websites
- Professional designs using extracted brand colors
- Mobile-responsive out of the box
- Edit with AI-powered prompts (describe changes in plain English)
- Publish with one click
- **Cost**: 10 credits ($0.20)

### Step 4: PITCH (Email)
**What it does**: Create personalized cold outreach
- AI-crafted emails tailored to each prospect
- Include pain points and value propositions
- Ready to send or customize
- **Cost**: 2 credits ($0.04)

**Complete workflow cost: 19 credits ($0.38)**

1. **Pay for what you use** - No wasted subscription fees

3. **Scalable** - Use more when busy, less when slow

# PART 5: FAQ FOR MARKETING MATERIALS

## Product Questions

**Q: What is RenovateMySite?**
A: RenovateMySite is an AI-powered platform that helps freelancers and agencies find local business leads, analyze their brands, build professional websites, and create personalized pitch emails - all from one intuitive dashboard.

**Q: Who is RenovateMySite for?**
A: Web design freelancers, digital marketing agencies, non-technical entrepreneurs, and anyone looking to offer website services to local businesses.

**Q: Do I need coding skills?**
A: Not at all. RenovateMySite is designed for non-technical users. Our AI does the technical work; you just provide the business context.

**Q: How is this different from [Wix/Squarespace/etc]?**
A: Those are website builders. RenovateMySite is a complete client acquisition platform. We help you find the leads, understand their brand, build a site to pitch them, AND write the outreach - all in minutes.

---

## Pricing Questions

**Q: How does the credit system work?**
A: Credits are like coins in an arcade. Each AI feature costs a certain number of credits. 1 credit = $0.02. You see the cost before each action, and can top up anytime.

**Q: Do credits expire?**
A: Trial credits expire after 14 days. Purchased credits never expire.

**Q: What's the cheapest way to try it?**
A: Start with the free 14-day trial (100 credits). That's enough for 5 complete client pitches.

**Q: Is there a subscription?**
A: The Beginner pack ($20) is one-time - no subscription. Agency 50 ($199/mo) is monthly for teams that need more volume and hosting.

**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**
A: Yes. The Beginner pack has no subscription to cancel. Agency 50 can be cancelled before the next billing cycle with no penalties.

**Q: What if I run out of credits?**
A: You'll see a prompt to purchase more. No surprise charges. You're always in control.

---

## Technical Questions

**Q: Where are the websites hosted?**
A: We use Firebase Hosting - the same infrastructure that powers Google. Fast, secure, and reliable.

**Q: Can I use my own domain?**
A: Yes! Connect any domain you own to your published sites. SSL certificates are included free.

**Q: How long does it take to build a website?**
A: The AI generates a complete, responsive website in 15-30 seconds. Editing and publishing add a few more minutes.

**Q: Can I edit the websites after generation?**
A: Absolutely. Just describe what you want to change in plain English and our AI makes the edits for you. No coding required.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes. We use Firebase/Google Cloud infrastructure with enterprise-grade security. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest.
